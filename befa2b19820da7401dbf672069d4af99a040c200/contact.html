About Me

I'm a creative mechanical designer that loves breaking down complex problems and finding simple and elegant solutions. I know the importance of exploring many ideas early in the design process and have the skills to quickly validate them with 3D modeling and prototyping.

I have six years of professional experience supporting projects through all stages of product development, including design research, ideation, mechanical design, machining and prototyping, and project management responsibilities.

I consider myself an avid learner both in and out of work. My hobbies include 3D printing, coding, carpentry, and outdoor recreation. For the past year I've spent much of my free time building a tiny house on wheels, which I hope to move into by the summer of 2021.

Thanks for viewing my work and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions.
